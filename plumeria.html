Our Story

The Best Place to
Enjoy Your Holiday

Nestled on the pristine shores of Thinadhoo Island in Vaavu Atoll, Plumeria Maldives offers an idyllic blend of affordable luxury and unparalleled comfort, making it one of the best-kept secrets in the Maldives.

With elegantly designed rooms that boast panoramic ocean views, an inviting indoor pool, and a private beach area, this boutique hotel provides a unique, intimate setting for those seeking a peaceful retreat. Plumeria Maldives delivers a luxurious experience without the hefty price tag, combining exceptional service, comfort, and breathtaking natural beauty.
